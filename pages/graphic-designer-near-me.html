Why does SEO matter? It's because of a little thing called Google (or Bing, if that's your style). When you search for a term, such as "graphic designer near me" a whole host of things come up. But how do I get to the #1 spot? Well, I've added that keyword phrase a few times in this blog post because I want Google to index me. I want my client to think: "This MUST be the place I need to be!"

  • header tags
  • paragraph copy: multiple uses
  • don't forget about the "alt" section in the img tags!
